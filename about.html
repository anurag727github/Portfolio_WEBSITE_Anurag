About me

"Hello, I'm from Pangire, a small village in Bhudargad district, Kop.
When I'm not exploring my hometown, you can find me on the sports field, indulging in my favorite games - Kabaddi, Cricket, and Kho-Kho.
I'm passionate about these sports and enjoy playing them with friends and family.
Kho-Kho, in particular, holds a special place in my heart, as it's a traditional Indian sport that's gaining popularity globally.
I'm excited to share my experiences and thoughts about these sports with you on this page.
